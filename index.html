E-Shop – Pawsonality Gear

A responsive e-commerce demo built with React (Vite) and client-side routing. Products and variant stock are loaded from Firebase Firestore, the cart persists in localStorage, and checkout is handled via Stripe Checkout created securely through a small Express backend. After payment, stock is reduced in Firestore using a transaction to avoid overselling.
